Hello, I'm Debangshu!

I am Debangshu Banerjee, currently pursuing my PhD at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) advised by Professor Aditya Gopalan. I am also a Student Researcher at Google DeepMind hosted by Dr. Karthikeyan Shanmugam. I have previously worked as a Research Intern at IBM Research Lab. My interests span general Machine Learning, Statistical Learning Theory, Online Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Large Language Model Alignment, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and Optimizing Computer Systems. I completed my Masters in Systems Science and Automation at IISc under Professor Shalabh Bhatnagar, specializing in Reinforcement Learning. During my brief but intense stint at AlphaICs, I worked on Deep Learning Compiler systems. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata. I am passionate about foundational understanding of modern data-centric learning systems and their real-world applications.
