Hello, I'm Debangshu!

I am Debangshu Banerjee, currently pursuing my PhD at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) adivised by Professor Aditya Gopalan. I am also a Student Researcher at Google DeepMind hosted by Dr. Karthikeyan Shanmugan. I have previously worked as a Research Intern at IBM Research Lab. My interests spans from general Machine Learning, Statistical Learning Theory, Online Learning, Reinforcement Learning to Large Language Model Alignment, Retrieval-Augmented Generation and Optimizing Computer Systems. Previously I pursued my Masters in Systems Science and Automation from IISc under Professor Shalabh Bhatnagar with a specialization in Reinforcememtn Learning. I have been fortunate enough to work on Deep Learning Compiler systems during my brief but intense stint at AlphaICs. I hold a Bachelors degree in Chemical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata. I am passionate about the foundational understanding of modern data-scentric learning systems along with their application and challenges in solving real-world problems.
